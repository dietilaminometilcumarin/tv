About Us

A Few Words About Me

Hello. I am the general producer of the TV channel and the ideological inspirer of the project.

I have many interests and professional inclinations. I am a certified programmer in the field of financial automation. Web designer and developer of business software products. I am developing virtual reality applications for mobile platforms: Apple, Android...

I write fiction as well as books on the technological development of Internet networks and related technologies.

I am actively involved in music. I play different musical instruments. I also write electronic instrumental music.

Having a huge interest in everything creative, I considered it necessary to combine all my inclinations and attach them to the work of other people. So that you can build a kind of bridge of interaction with similar people. Thus, the idea of creating a private television was born. I hope to find the same creative people as myself and become useful to each other.

What We Offer

Our TV channel is a window into the world of music, cinema, art, sports, healthy lifestyle and all other types of human activity that contribute to the development of the individual and the world around.
